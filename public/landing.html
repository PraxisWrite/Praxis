The student experience

A guided path from idea to submission

Every assignment follows the same structured flow — so students build good habits, and teachers always know where each student is.

💡
Step 1

Get ideas

An AI writing coach helps students plan and build their outline — asking questions rather than writing for them.

✍️
Step 2

Write the draft

Students write their draft with a live word count and optional AI feedback, limited to a teacher-set number of requests.

🔍
Step 3

Review and finalise

Students revise their draft into a final version. The coach is locked — the thinking has to show in the writing.

✅
Step 4

Submit and reflect

Before submitting, students self-assess against the rubric. Their ratings sit alongside the teacher's — honestly.
